How "For You" works

What is For You?
Instead of showing articles in reverse-chronological order, "For You" ranks every article by how likely you are to find it valuable — based on your reading habits and reactions.

Signals we use

  • 👍👎 Reactions — thumbs up/down are the strongest signal
  • ⭐ Stars — treated as a strong like (2× weight)
  • 📖 Read here — opening an article inline is a strong engagement signal (0.5× weight). Works for both articles and embedded videos.
  • 🔗 External link clicks — weak positive signal (0.3× weight)
  • ⏰ Freshness — newer articles get a small boost
  • 🔎 Exploration — unrated sources get a diversity boost to prevent filter bubbles

Why you might see an article

  • From a source you like — you've liked articles from this feed
  • Matches your interests — you've liked articles in this topic cluster
  • Related to: keyword — title keywords match your history
  • Discover new content — this source hasn't been rated yet

How to improve your feed
React with 👍/👎 as you read, or use + / - keyboard shortcuts. Opening articles with "Read here" (i) also contributes — both for text articles and embedded videos. The algorithm updates immediately — a few dozen interactions is enough to see meaningful personalisation.
